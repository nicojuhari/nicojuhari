# Case Study: Loan & Finance Management Web App
**Industry:** Consumer Finance | **Type:** Excel replacement → Custom Web App

---

## The Problem

A consumer finance company was managing all loan tracking, client data, income, and expenses in Excel.

One person handled everything manually — entering client data by hand, updating records one by one. Generating any kind of report meant digging through rows and making rough calculations. The team had no real visibility into daily or monthly performance — only rough totals. And the data? Locked to one computer, accessible to one person.

As the business grew, the Excel became the bottleneck.

---

## The Solution

I replaced the entire Excel system with a custom web application built for how the business actually operates.

**What I built:**
- Loan tracking with full client history — no more re-entering data
- Income and expense management with automated calculations
- Daily and monthly reports with real numbers, not assumptions
- Multi-user access — any team member can view or work with data
- Accessible from any device, anywhere, 24/7

---

## The Outcome

- **Reports went from zero to instant** — daily and monthly views available in one click
- **Manual data entry eliminated** — client info entered once, reused everywhere
- **The whole team got access** — no longer dependent on one person
- **Business decisions became data-driven** — management could finally see exactly where they stood
- **Available on any phone or laptop** — no more being tied to one machine

The company could now operate and grow without the system becoming a bigger problem every month.
