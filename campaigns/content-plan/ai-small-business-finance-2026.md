## Recommended Outline

**H1: AI for Small Business Finance in 2026: What Actually Works (vs. What's Just Hype)**

**Intro:** 82% of small businesses have invested in AI tools. Most of them are using AI to draft emails and summarize meetings. That's fine — but it's not where the real leverage is for business finance and operations. This is what actually moves the needle.

**H2: What AI Does Well for Business Finance**
Specific, honest list:
- Invoice processing and data extraction (AI reads invoices, extracts amounts, logs them)
- Cash flow prediction (if the data is clean — that's the key caveat)
- Recurring report generation (daily/weekly summaries without someone building them manually)
- Anomaly detection (flagging unusual transactions without a dedicated analyst)
- Document summarization (contracts, loan agreements, financial statements)

**H2: What AI Can't Do (Yet) — and Where It Gets Oversold**
- AI can't fix bad data. If your data is in Excel, scattered across files, AI downstream gives bad output
- AI tools won't replace a system you don't have — they amplify what's already there
- Most "AI for SMBs" tools are AI-wrapped versions of tools you already use
- The gap: AI needs structured data to work on. Many SMBs don't have that yet.

**H2: The Honest Stack for Small Business Finance in 2026**
A practical framework — not a list of 30 tools:
1. **Get your data structured first** — This is the custom software / Excel-replacement step
2. **Add AI on top** — Once data is clean and accessible, AI tools work well
3. **Automate the reporting layer** — AI can generate daily/weekly views from clean data

The mistake most SMBs make: buying AI tools before fixing their data problem.

**H2: When to Build Custom vs. When to Buy AI Tools**
Decision framework (this is the finance-developer perspective):
- Off-the-shelf AI works when: process is standard, data is already structured, you want to save time on known tasks
- Custom software is right when: your process is non-standard, your data is stuck in Excel, no off-the-shelf tool fits how you work
- The hybrid: build the structured system first → add AI features on top (AI-assisted reporting, anomaly alerts)

**H2: What I've Seen Work in Practice**
Brief practitioner perspective — drawn from the consumer finance case study and general experience:
- Replacing Excel with structured web app → instant reports → owner makes decisions in seconds
- Adding AI document processing to a loan management system → application review 3× faster
- The pattern: custom foundation + AI layer = actual leverage
