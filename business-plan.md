# Business Plan — nicojuhari.com
**Goal:** Get paying IT clients ASAP. Build toward stable €3-5k+/month freelance income.

---

## Positioning

**Who:** Nicolae Cojuhari — software engineer & digital solutions partner for Austrian SMEs and startups.

**For whom:** Small and medium businesses in Vienna (and remotely across DACH) that are:
- Running on outdated websites that don't convert
- Still using Excel/spreadsheets for things that should be apps
- Doing repetitive manual work that should be automated
- Selling online and need a proper Shopify setup

**One-sentence pitch:**
> "I build websites, web apps, and automation tools that save you time and make you money — in Vienna and remote."

**Not:** A developer for hire by the hour. A solutions partner paid for outcomes.

---

## Services + Pricing

| Service | What it is | Price range |
|---|---|---|
| **Business Website** | Fast, modern, SEO-ready site for SMEs | €1,500 – €3,000 |
| **Ecommerce (Shopify)** | Full Shopify setup, custom theme, payments | €2,500 – €6,000 |
| **Excel Killer App** | Replace spreadsheets with a proper web tool (inventory, client mgmt, finance, HR) | €2,500 – €8,000 |
| **Process Automation** | Eliminate manual workflows (forms, emails, reports, integrations) | €1,500 – €4,000 |
| **CRO / Redesign** | Improve existing site to convert better | €1,000 – €2,500 |
| **Monthly Retainer** | Ongoing support, updates, hosting mgmt | €300 – €800/mo |

**The Excel Killer is the highest-leverage offer.** Every Vienna SME — Handwerker, clinics, law firms, restaurants, retailers — is running something critical in Excel. They don't know what's possible or what it costs. Walk in with "I'll replace your spreadsheet with a tool your whole team can use on any device" — that's a €3-6k project with massive perceived value and zero competition from cheap freelancers on Fiverr.

## Additional angles

- **AI integrations** — hot demand, premium pricing. Add AI features to existing tools (chatbots, document processing, automation). Most SMEs are asking about AI but don't know where to start.
- **White-label / agency subcontracting** — be the dev behind Vienna agencies. They already have clients, you do the work. Fastest path to first invoice.
- **Handwerker-Netz crossover** — clients from the handyman business who need websites or tools. Warm leads, trust already built.

## Website Direction

**New headline focus:** Business outcomes, not tech stack.
- OLD: "I create pixel-perfect web apps using Shopify and modern JavaScript / Vue / Nuxt"
- NEW direction: "I build web solutions that grow your business" or "Websites, web apps, and automations for businesses that mean it"

**Key sections:**
1. Hero — clear offer + WhatsApp + contact CTA
2. Services — 3-4 packages with clear scope
3. Work / Case studies — 2-3 real examples with results
4. Tech stack — brief, not the focus
5. Tools — keeps the site interesting and shows skills
6. Contact — WhatsApp first, form second

**Language:** Consider a German version or German-first for local Vienna SME outreach.

## Income Math

To hit €3,000/month:
- 1 website + 1 retainer = doable
- 1 Excel Killer project alone covers it
- Agency subcontracting at €50-70/hr = 50 hours/month

To hit €5,000/month:
- 2 active projects or 1 large project + 2 retainers
- Fully achievable solo within 3-6 months
